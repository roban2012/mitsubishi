Petro-Diamond Inc. 石油的销售 美国

Petro-Diamond Inc. (PDI) was established in 1983 for the purpose of marketing and distributing petroleum transportation fuels in Southern California including CARB gasoline, low sulfur diesel, ethanol, and marine fuels. The clean petroleum products are staged and distributed from a modern terminal facility (PDTC) located in the Port of Long Beach.

PDI's Clean Products Department specializes in providing the finest quality petroleum products which meet all California State requirements for clean burning fuels. PDI's customer base includes "big box" retailers, independent wholesalers, transportation districts, and other government agencies. All PDI clean products can be delivered to the Customer's retail location or FOB the PDTC truck rack in Long Beach. PDI also offers clear on-highway diesel and red-dyed diesel.

PDI is a major supplier of ethanol for the blending of gasoline at outlying terminals by other gasoline marketers and blenders operating in Southern California.

The Marine Fuels Department offers fueling services to vessels via barge in the Ports of Los Angeles and Long Beach.

The Petro-Diamond Terminal facility in Long Beach provides PDI the unique opportunity of being able to supply its Customers quality transportation fuels 24 hours per day, 365 days per year. The facility is supplied by vessel and pipeline from local Southern California refineries. The base products are sequentially blended and injected with additives into trucks at the 4-lane truck loading rack.

Astomos Energy Corporation 液化石油气的进口和贸易、国内物流、销售 日本

On April 1, 2006, Idemitsu Kosan and Mitsubishi Corporation merged the Idemitsu Gas and Life Co., Ltd. and Mitsubishi Liquified Gas Co., Ltd. to form Astomos Energy Corporation, based on a common desire to "create a completely new and competitive energy company by consolidating all facets of LPG import, trading, domestic distribution and sales so that the company can leverage its larger scale and synergy." Through this merging Astomos became the largest LPG-specialized supplier in the world, and the top LPG company in Japan commanding 20% of the domestic share.

　LP gas is one of the backbones of Japan's energy supply used by more than half of the approximately 50 million households. Similar to natural gas, LP gas is a clean and environmentally friendly form of energy. And its distributed supply is much more resilient to natural disasters. LP gas is positioned in Japan's Basic Energy Plan as a form of energy whose demand should be increased into the future.

　Astomos Energy is committed to providing our customers' homes and businesses with a warm future with reliable life by leveraging the effects of this merging that allows us to improve our LPG supply capabilities, consolidate human resources, and expand our sales and supply networks that we create with our dealerships and business partners.

Please expect great things from Astomos Energy in the present and in the future.
