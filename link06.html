Isuzu Corporation 钢材加工及销售业务 日本

五十铃以前瞻性企业*为目标。具体地说就是展开“可以心情愉快地工作，工作业绩优秀，可以自我成长的企业”，并作为地球公民，立志以独特的组织形式为世界作出贡献。

战后，以日本的高度经济成长为背景，五十铃从钢铁薄板批发开始突飞猛进。现在以钢板服务中心为核心，实践扩大物流、解决方案、生活服务机能的集团经营。

五十铃最大的优势就是“人与组织”，及其观点与思想。因此“组织”的价值与“人才”的价值自律性得以提高，坚持不懈地持续实践两者创发性地融和而产生企业价值的经营。

现在，五十铃以坚强的创业者领导能力从“报时”时代开始，焕然一新地发展到全体员工参画的“时钟制造”设计者的集团。实践重视创业者精神的理念经营，同时持续不断地向前进化，向从服务中心到以世界为舞台的“价值中心”的挑战已经拉开序幕。

我们的原动力，就是“我们的理想”的宏伟大志。

此次，制定了十年后的长期经营目标，应该努力的方向已经明确。在国际化的21世纪，我们认为应该以对股东、客户、公司员工、世界上所有利益相关者的成长与发展做出贡献为使命，持续成为创业者精神之一的“社会上不可或缺的一分子”。

以前瞻性企业为目标的五十铃，每个人都在积极主动地体现其精神背景的理念。让我们揭开做为理念的“社训”的深远含意，以及在继承社训的同时培育企业文化的历史长卷。

五十铃集团自1988年起实行分社制，进一步强化与市场 紧密相连的服务体制。自2001年起，采用现在的四个部门体制，使对钢板流通应发挥的功能、服务更加明确，努力创造价值。

同时，谋求资本与经营的分离，引入执行董事制度，以期强化公司治理。

五十铃的董事会进行有关集团经营的决议。执行董事会决定执行责任人，进行经营的执行，还设置各委员会，追求执行事业活动的健全性与高效性。

Triland Metals Ltd. 伦敦金属交易所（LME）经纪人业务 英国

Triland Metals is a London-based broker and market maker, specialising in the futures trading of non-ferrous metals. As a long established Ring Dealing Member of the London Metal Exchange and a wholly owned subsidiary of the Mitsubishi Corporation, Triland is perfectly positioned to understand and respond to the needs of its customers around the world.

In addition to our broking, market making and risk management solutions, Triland provides customers with options, averaging and warrant services as well as up-to-date market information and research.

One company, with forty years of experience, working with customers in forty two countries across five continents. Dealing in three time zones, fluent in eight languages and with one over-riding purpose: helping customers to manage the risks of metals trading. Welcome to the facts and figures of Triland.

Triland Metals is a subsidiary of Mitsubishi Corporation (MC). MC is Japan's largest general trading company with over 200 bases of operations in approximately 80 countries worldwide. With over 500 group companies, MC employs a multinational workforce of approximately 54,000 people. MC has long been engaged in business with customers around the world in virtually every industry, including energy, metals, machinery, chemicals, food and general merchandise.

In 1971 MC saw the need to set up a specialist metals trading company to hedge against price movements in the metals markets. A triple alliance took place between companies in London, New York and Tokyo, and Triland Metals was born. The main office is in London and our subsidiaries are Triland Metals Americas Inc and Triland Metals Tokyo, giving us presence on the ground in key markets and in the three key time zones around the world.

Triland Metals customers are spread all over the world from Belgium to Brazil; from Chile to China and from Switzerland to South Korea. Our dealing team is multi-lingual, with eight languages spoken fluently including Japanese, Mandarin and most European languages, and we are available to trade whenever our customers need us.

Furuya Metal Co., Ltd. 贵金属加工业 日本

As our society becomes more information-driven, our lifestyles and industries are undergoing deep change. At the same time, environmental and energy conservation problems demand a global response. Furuya Metal monitors social and industrial change closely. Since our founding, contributing to the advance of science and technology and the prosperity of society through our industrial precious-metal products, especially platinum, has been part of our corporate philosophy.

Furuya Metal’s core products consist of precious metals. Among them are the platinum group metals (PGM, including platinum, iridium and ruthenium), gold, and silver. These outstanding metals tolerate very hot temperatures, are chemically stable, offer high electrical conductivity, and more. They support the development of leading-edge technologies like smartphones, tablets and other mobile devices, and memory media like hard disks. These amazing materials make medical testing equipment, touch panel displays, LEDs, OLEDs, and chemical catalysts possible.

Output, however, is unevenly distributed, with the Republic of South Africa by far the largest producing country. And since overall production is also limited, precious metals are a very expensive mineral resource. Furuya Metal works with South African mining companies to form stable, direct supply routes for precious mineral resources. The technical know-how we have nurtured since our founding helps us make high-quality products. And we work constantly to provide a stable, optimally timed flow of products to our customers.

The applications of precious metals are expanding all the time. They are at work in the fields of electronic devices, semiconductors, the environment, clean energy, medicine, and more. Going forward, we are actively improving precious metal recycling technology to meet the demands of society and industry. Our research and development is exploring the potential of platinum, iridium, ruthenium, and other PGMs. Our new materials and technologies are key to developing state-of-the-art technology for future generations.

I look forward to your ongoing support so that we may prosper together.
